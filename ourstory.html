Our Story

Our journey began with friendship and countless cups of kaapi shared during engineering days. Long hours, late nights, and unfinished dreams slowly brewed an idea — to create a space where people could pause, connect, and restart. On January 1, 2025, that idea took shape as Error 404: KAAPI Not Found. At a time when life moves fast and moments feel skipped, we set out to bring back the comfort of a perfectly brewed cup. What started as casual conversations among six friends became a shared mission — to serve kaapi that feels familiar, honest, and grounding. Built by engineers and driven by passion, our café blends precision with warmth. Every cup is brewed with care, every detail with intention. More than just coffee, this is a place to slow down, recharge, and feel at home. Error 404: KAAPI Not Found is where lost moments reconnect, and every visit ends with the same result — KAAPI. FOUND. ☕
